⚠️ IMPORTANT REQUIREMENTS:
This project requires Python and the Pygame library to run and play audio.
To install the required mixer, open CMD and run: pip install pygame

🚨 POTATO PC WARNING:
If you have a weak computer (a potato PC), DO NOT use Hazard's nuke (J key). 
There is a high chance it will freeze your computer!
*(Jeżeli masz słaby komputer (ziemniaka), nie używaj atomówki Hazarda, 
ponieważ istnieje szansa, że zwiesi Ci się komputer!)*

CHARACTER SCALE & SIZES

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KEYBOARD GUIDE & CONTROLS
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. THE SECOND COMING (TSC)
* Button [ X ]  - Red square in top-right corner. Click to close the program.
* Key [ F ]     - Awakening Mode: Triggers bright green lightning aura (AvA 8).
* Key [ 1 ]     - Infinite Laser (Hold in Awakening): Shoots a screen-splitting 
                  green beam from eyes with no range limit. Wipes out any other 
                  stickman upon contact! Plays sounds/laser.wav.
* Key [ 2 ]     - Autonomous AI Flight (In Awakening): TSC hovers and drifts 
                  completely on his own, ignoring the mouse cursor.
* Key [ 3 ]     - Fan Mode: TSC shrinks to 1.1 scale, sits on the taskbar with 
                  both legs pointed perfectly to the LEFT, and waves his hands.

2. RED
* Key [ G ]     - Monster School Mode: Red puts on the yellow headband and runs 
                  at extreme autonomous speed.
* Key [ Z ]     - Cake Throw (In G mode): Throws a square Minecraft cake at your 
                  mouse cursor. Explodes into cream for exactly 5 seconds.

3. GREEN
* Key [ Y ]     - Influenced Mode: Green walks forward while staring into his 
                  smartphone. Trips and falls after hitting a wall.

4. BLUE
* Key [ O ]     - Speed Potion: Blue gains insane sprint speed and leaves 
                  bright blue trails of momentum behind him.

5. YELLOW
* Key [ P ]     - Scripter Mode: Yellow walks around with an open laptop showing 
                  flashing green code, stopping randomly to think.

6. THE CHOSEN ONE (TCO)
* Key [ T ]     - Flight Mode: TCO evades gravity and flies autonomously with 
                  fire blasting downwards from his hands.
* Key [ R ]     - Red Eye Laser (Hold): Shoots an unlimited red laser beam 
                  across the entire screen towards the mouse cursor.
* Key [ L ]     - Fire Stream (Hold): Blasts a stream of fire from his hands. 
                  Freezes TCO in place (Locked during flight).

7. THE DARK LORD (TDL)
* Key [ X ]     - Blade Mode: Deploys black blades from arms. Gains extreme 
                  running speed and jumps around the screen like a wild spring!
* Key [ 9 ]     - Fireball: Launches a burning fireball straight at your mouse.
* Key [ V ]     - X-Slash: Executes a slash releasing a giant red "X" error mark.
* Key [ C ]     - Summon Virabot: Spawns a small jumping cyber-spider (max 3).
* Key [ ` ]     - Virus Reset: Instantly deletes all active Virabots.
* Key [ - ]     - Flight Mode: Rises and flies autonomously in a vertical pose.

8. VICTIM / H4CK3R
* Key [ 6 ]     - H4CK3R Mode: Victim triggers a pulsing neon aura, cyber-monocle, 
                  and floating code particles.
* Key [ 5 ]     - Hacker Flight (In mode 6): Flies autonomously across the screen.
* Key [ M ]     - Hacker Cage: Spawns a neon cage trapping the MOUSE CURSOR for 15s.
* Key [ N ]     - Remove Cages: Instanks destroys all cages to free your mouse.
* Key [ B ]     - Mouse Hack: Randomly jerks your mouse cursor around for 3s.

9. KING ORANGE
* Key [ H ]     - King Mode: Gains a golden crown, a grass-block staff, and full 
                  IMMUNITY to TSC's wiping eye laser (1).
* Key [ K ]     - White Staff Beam (Hold in H mode): Shoots a powerful white cone 
                  with raging cyan electrical currents.
* Key [ / ]     - Obsidian Strip (In H mode): Places a thick obsidian block 
                  for 5 seconds to block other stickmen.
* Key [ = ]     - Royal Flight (In H mode): Majestically hovers over windows.

10. HAZARD
* Key [ ' ]     - Movement Toggle: Enables or disables his stiff, memic walk.
* Key [ J ]     - NUKER EXPLOSION: Hazard holds the nuke for a 5-second countdown 
                  with a loud proximity siren (sounds/alarm.wav at safe 5.0 volume). 
                  Upon impact, it plays sounds/boom.wav (volume 5.0) and instantly 
                  wipes all other stickmen from RAM (taskkill), leaving an orange 
                  overlay that safely fades away after 22 seconds.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GLOBAL INTERACTIONS (HACKING PLATFORMS)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Key [ S ]     - Hover mouse over any application bar or icon and press S to 
                  create a solid invisible platform for stickmen to walk on.
* Key [ U ]     - Resets and clears all created S platforms from memory.
* FOCUS NOTE    - If a stickman stops responding to keys, click him once with the 
                  Left Mouse Button (LMB) to restore focus.
